About

DesignSouth Creative Conference brings the best and the brightest in the contemporary design landscape to share their experiences and inspire budding and seasoned designers alike in a day of talks, exhibits, demonstrations, merchandise and more.

Mindanao has long been producing world-class artists and designers. This will be a venue grand enough for all this talent to gather, showcase their work, and learn from each other.
